## 1.引入redis依赖
````
<!--redis-->
<dependency>
    <groupId>org.springframework.boot</groupId>
    <artifactId>spring-boot-starter-data-redis</artifactId>
</dependency>
````

## 2.配置redis
````
spring:
  redis:
      database: 1
      host: 47.115.41.69
      port: 6379
      password: zhengXIN@123
````
